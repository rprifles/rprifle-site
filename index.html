FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What types of rifles do you build?

Ridgeline Precision Rifles builds hunting rifles, muzzleloaders, and competition-oriented rifles for PRS, NRL, and ELR applications.

Do you offer gunsmithing services on existing rifles?

Yes. Services include rebarreling, chambering, action blueprinting, muzzle work, trigger work, optic setup, and load development support.

How do appointments work?

The business currently operates by appointment only, which helps provide a more direct and focused experience for each project.

Can I contact you about a custom project?

Yes. Customers can reach out by phone or email to discuss a rifle build, service request, or component-related project.
